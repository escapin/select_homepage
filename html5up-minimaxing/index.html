sElect: A Lightweight Verifiable Remote Voting System

welcome to sElect

sElect (secure/simple elections) is a remote voting system meant for low-risk elections. It is designed to be particularly simple and lightweight in terms of its structure, its user experience, and the cryptography it uses. sElect is not meant to defend against coercion and mostly tries to defend against untrusted or malicious authorities, including inadvertent programming errors or deliberate manipulation of servers, but excluding targeted and sophisticated attacks against votersâ€™ devices.
