sElect: A Lightweight Verifiable Remote Voting System

welcome to sElect

sElect is a remote voting system enabling your vote to remain confidential while providing the possibility to verify that your ballot has been properly counted.

sElect is designed to be particularly simple and lightweight in terms of its structure, its user experience, and the cryptography it uses.

We performed a rigorous cryptographic analysis of the sElect's underlying protocol showing that, despite its simplicity, it enjoys a good level of privacy, verifiability, and accountability.

Try the demo

main features

  • Fully Automated Verification

    Triggered as soon as the voter looks at the election result, it performs cryptographic checks to make sure that her vote has been properly counted.

  • Human Verifiability

    The voter can check whether a verification code she has obtanied when casting her vote appears in the election result along with her choice.

  • Simple Cryptography and Design

    Unlike other e-voting systems, sElect employs only public key encryption and digital signature, leaving open the possibility to use post-quantum cryptography, if it becomes necessarily.
